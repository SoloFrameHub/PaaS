Frequently Asked Questions

I'm an engineer, not a salesperson. Is this for me?

This was built specifically for you. You don't need charisma - you need a protocol. We teach you to debug buyer pain points using discovery frameworks that work like logic gates. Every course is quick-consumption: learn a framework, practice with AI, build an artifact.

How do cohort sprints work?

Monthly 4-week sprints rotate through tracks on a 6-month cycle. You get self-paced access to all courses plus structured weekly rhythms with your cohort. Accountability pods of 4-5 founders review each other's work, share wins, and keep you building.

What's included?

All 49 courses across 7 tracks are live: Foundations, Marketing Engine, Sales Methodology, AI-Powered Acquisition, Creator Economy, Customer Success, and Operations & Systems. Everything from sales psychology through scaling to your first hire - plus unlimited AI roleplay, cohort sprints, and accountability pods.

How is the AI roleplay different from ChatGPT?

Our 3D persona system combines your founder type, target industry (80+ options), and DISC buyer personality to generate realistic, contextual responses. It tracks methodology adherence, evaluates your performance, and provides coaching - not just conversation.

Do I need to keep up with the cohort?

You always have self-paced access to every course. Cohort sprints are structured learning experiences that accelerate progress - they're there to help you, not pressure you. Miss a sprint? Join the next one.

What's the difference from the Playbook?

The Playbook gives you the strategic frameworks (MAGNETS, DISC, MVQ, AI Outreach). The OS gives you hands-on execution - AI roleplay, artifact builders, cohort collaboration, and real-time coaching. Frameworks in the book, execution in the OS.
