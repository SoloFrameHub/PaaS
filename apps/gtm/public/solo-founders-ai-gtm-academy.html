Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to implement all 8 systems at once?

No. The OS includes a Business Stage Evaluation that helps you prioritize which systems to implement first based on your current situation. Most founders start with 2-3 systems.

What's the difference between this and the Solo GTM OS?

The Solo GTM OS teaches you how to sell. The GTM Track teaches you how to build systems that generate and convert leads at scale, often without your direct involvement.

How technical do I need to be for the Automation Flywheel?

Everything is no-code. If you can use tools like Zapier, Make, or n8n, you have the technical skills needed. The module walks you through every integration step-by-step.

Is this only for SaaS businesses?

No. The 8 systems work for any business model - productized services, info products, e-commerce, consulting. The principles of compounding growth are universal.
