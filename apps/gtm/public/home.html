Frequently Asked Questions

What is the weekly time commitment?
Expect to spend about 3–5 hours per week. This includes learning the framework modules (Monday), practicing with the AI (Tue/Wed), and executing real calls (Thursday/Friday).
Do I need a product to join?
Yes. This is an execution OS, not a theoretical course. You need something to sell, even if it's just an MVP or a service, so you can build real artifacts and handle real objections.
Is this just a video course?
No. This isn't a course - it's an AI-Powered Operating System. 13 AI task types, voice-enabled roleplay against 4 DISC buyer personalities, Claude-powered coaching, ICP builder workbench, AI-graded assessments, and a pipeline state machine that responds to your deals in real time.
What if I'm an introvert/engineer?
You are exactly who this is built for. The OS treats sales as a system of inputs and outputs, not a charisma contest. You don't need charisma - you need a protocol. Start by practicing with AI buyer personas in a safe, consequence-free environment.
What's the difference between the book and the OS?
The Customer Acquisition Playbook teaches the strategic frameworks (MAGNETS, DISC, Discovery, MVQ). The OS provides hands-on execution with voice-enabled AI roleplay, ICP builder workbench, Claude-powered coaching, and AI-graded assessments. Frameworks in the book, execution in the OS.
What is the Quick Win Path?
After completing the free Readiness Score, the OS identifies your lowest-scoring GTM dimension and surfaces exactly 3 lessons targeted at that gap. Designed to deliver a usable output — a sharper ICP, a first cold email, a discovery call framework — within 90 minutes of joining.
Can I connect my own CRM and email tools?
Yes. Attio CRM (pipeline sync + contact enrichment) and Hunter.io (email finding by domain) both connect via your own API key — BYOK, no markup. Results stay in-browser; we never store contact data. Notion export is also available. All integrations are opt-in.
Is there a certification?
Yes. Complete Track 1 (Foundations) + Track 3 (Sales Methodology) + a roleplay session scoring ≥ 75% to earn the Certified Solo GTM Practitioner badge. It's issued via Badgr (Open Badges 3.0) and is verifiable on LinkedIn. The gate is real — passing requires demonstrated skill, not just course completion.
¿Está disponible en español?

What is the refund policy?
We offer a "Do The Work" Beta Checkpoint Promise. Starting from the day billing begins, if within 30 days you complete the onboarding, 10 roleplay sessions, and 5 real discovery calls and still don't feel the system is valuable, request a full refund.
