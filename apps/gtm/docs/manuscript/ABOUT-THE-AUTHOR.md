# About the Author

![Mike Sullivan — author, creator, solo founder](visuals/mike-sullivan-author-creator-solo-founder.jpg)

**Mike Sullivan** has spent three decades turning technology into revenue. He started in GE's Technical Marketing Program, then led a 20-person sales and delivery team at Unisys to 325% of quota. From there he moved into venture-backed startups: AirDefense (100% year-over-year growth), AeroScout (SVP Strategy), and CIC (approximately $1B valuation). Along the way he built strategic partnerships with Sony-Ericsson, Motorola, HP, Intel, and IBM, and holds two patents in wireless security.

For the past fourteen years he has worked independently on Upwork with 100% client satisfaction, consulting for founders on the same customer acquisition challenges this book addresses. One engagement built a psychiatric practice's web presence and SEO from zero to four practitioners through organic acquisition alone.

Mike considers himself a semi-technical founder—engineering degree, but his career has been in strategy, customer acquisition, and turning technology into revenue. He builds and runs his own stack using a code editor with API access, Claude Code, Cursor, and AI assistants—not as a full-time engineer, but as a founder who refuses to be blocked by technical gaps.

As AI began removing friction from the startup process, Mike developed the frameworks that became the backbone of SoloFrameHub, an AI-native education platform for solo and semi-technical founders. He is building in public (see Chapter 15), releasing books with matching Course Academies and documenting the process from book writing to course creation to platform development.
