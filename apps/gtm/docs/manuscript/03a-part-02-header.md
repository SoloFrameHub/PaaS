# PART II

## Conversations & Conversion

You know why selling feels hard. You know who you're targeting. You know where to find them and how to start conversations. Now comes the part that turns interest into revenue.

**Chapter 4** covers discovery calls—how to diagnose real problems instead of collecting pleasantries, and how to qualify hard so you stop wasting time on "maybes."

**Chapter 5** tackles the moment most founders dread: naming a price. You'll learn to present your solution and talk about money without flinching or discounting reflexively.

**Chapter 6** addresses what most acquisition books ignore—retention and expansion. The cheapest customer to acquire is the one you already have. You'll build a system for referrals, renewals, and account growth.

**Chapter 7** shows you how to use AI and automation to multiply your capacity without losing the human touch that wins deals at this price point.

## What You'll Have By the End

- A discovery call framework that uncovers real problems and buying intent
- Qualification criteria that save you from wasting time on "maybes"
- Scripts and techniques for pricing conversations that feel confident, not desperate
- A retention system that turns customers into advocates and referral sources
- AI-assisted workflows that let you operate at scale without a team

Everything in Part I prepared you for this. Everything in Part III will help you systematize it.
