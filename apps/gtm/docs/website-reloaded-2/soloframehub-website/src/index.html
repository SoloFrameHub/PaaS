Frequently Asked Questions

What is the weekly time commitment?
Expect to spend about 3–5 hours per week. This includes learning the framework modules (Monday), practicing with the AI (Tue/Wed), and executing real calls (Thursday/Friday).
Do I need a product to join?
Yes. This is an execution academy, not a theoretical one. You need something to sell, even if it's just an MVP or a service, so you can build real artifacts and handle real objections.
Is this just a video course?
No. There is no "sit-and-watch" video course here. The academy is a highly interactive, AI-native execution environment: AI roleplay sessions, artifact builders, and a pipeline state machine that responds to your deals in real time.
What if I'm an introvert/engineer?
You are exactly who this is built for. We treat sales as a system of inputs and outputs, not a charisma contest. You start by practicing with AI buyer personas in a safe, consequence-free environment.
What's the difference between the book and the academy?
The Customer Acquisition Playbook teaches the strategic frameworks (MAGNETS, DISC, Discovery). The Academy provides hands-on practice with AI roleplay, artifact builders, and real-time coaching. Frameworks in the book, execution in the academy.
What is the refund policy?
We offer a "Do The Work" Beta Checkpoint Promise. Starting from the day billing begins, if within 30 days you complete the onboarding, 10 roleplay sessions, and 5 real discovery calls and still don't feel the system is valuable, request a full refund.
