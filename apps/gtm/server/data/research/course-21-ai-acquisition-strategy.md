## LESSON 4: Lead Scoring & Intent Signal Detection (50 min)

### Key Topics

1. **Solo Founder Lead Scoring Model (1-10)** — Three dimensions: Fit (ICP match), Signal (behavioral intent), Friction (barriers)
2. **Fit Scoring (0-4 points)** — Firmographic match, title, company size, tech stack
3. **Signal Scoring (0-4 points)** — Job change, funding, content engagement, website visit
4. **Friction Scoring (0-2, subtracted)** — Long sales cycle industry, committee buying
5. **Intent Signal Detection Platforms** — What's available at solo founder budgets
6. **Automating Score Updates** — Zapier/Make for score updates based on engagement

### Data Points & Statistics

| Statistic | Source |
|-----------|--------|
| Job changes: 3x more likely to buy within 90 days | LinkedIn Sales Solutions |
| Recently funded companies: 2-5x more likely to invest in new tools | Crunchbase data |
| Lead scoring increases sales productivity by 20-30% | Forrester / HubSpot |
| Only 25% of marketing leads are sales-ready | Gleanster Research |
| 50%+ of sales time wasted on unqualified prospects without scoring | InsideSales.com |

### Frameworks & Models

- **FIT + SIGNAL + FRICTION Model**:
  - Fit (0-4): +1 each for industry, title, company size, tech stack match
  - Signal (0-4): +1 each for job change, funding, content engagement, competitor eval
  - Friction (0 to -2): -1 each for long sales cycle, committee buying
  - Action thresholds: 8-10 = Immediate personal outreach; 5-7 = Automated sequence; 1-4 = Nurture or disqualify

- **Intent Signal Hierarchy**: First-party (free: website, emails), Second-party (low cost: LinkedIn, news), Third-party (expensive, NOT recommended: Bombora $2K+/mo)

## LESSON 6: The Automation Failure Matrix (55 min)

### Key Topics

1. **The Automation Failure Matrix (2x2)** — Risk of Failure × Time Savings
2. **The Four Quadrants**: Q1 Automate Now, Q2 Automate + Human Gate, Q3 Keep Human, Q4 Eliminate
3. **Where AI Outreach Backfires** — Hallucinated personalization, LinkedIn bans
4. **The "Uncanny Valley" of AI Sales**
5. **Human-in-the-Loop Design Patterns**
6. **Compliance and Reputation Risks**

### Frameworks & Models

**The Automation Failure Matrix:**

|  | Low Risk of Failure | High Risk of Failure |
|--|---------------------|---------------------|
| **High Time Savings** | **Q1: AUTOMATE NOW** — Data entry, warmup, verification, CRM updates, reminders | **Q2: AUTOMATE + HUMAN GATE** — AI personalization, scoring, chatbot qualification, content drafts |
| **Low Time Savings** | **Q4: ELIMINATE** — Manual reporting you don't act on, formatting perfectionism | **Q3: KEEP HUMAN** — Discovery calls, pricing/negotiation, top-20% outreach, relationship repair |

**HITL Decision Framework**: For each automation: (1) Worst case if wrong? (2) Can I detect error before prospect sees it? (3) Recovery cost?

### Frameworks & Models

**Implementation Sprint (7-14 Days):**

| Day | Activity | Output |
|-----|----------|--------|
| 1-2 | Configure tools | Tools connected |
| 3 | Build 50-prospect list | Scored list in CRM |
| 4-5 | Create sequences (2 segments) | Sequences loaded |
| 6 | Chatbot + Zapier automations | Website qualification live |

**Pipeline Math:**
Revenue Goal / Deal Size = Deals Needed → / Close Rate = Conversations Needed → / Reply-to-Conversation Rate = Replies Needed → / Reply Rate = Emails Needed.
Example: $120K/year, $5K deal = 24 deals = 2/month. At 25% close = 8 conversations. At 10% reply rate = 160 emails/month.

**5-Metric Dashboard:**
1. Prospects Added/Week (target: 20-50)
2. Emails Sent/Week (target: 150-500)
3. Reply Rate (target: 5-15%)
4. Conversations Booked/Week (target: 2-5)
5. Pipeline Value/Month
