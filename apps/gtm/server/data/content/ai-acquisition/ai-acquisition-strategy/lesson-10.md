## Your System Blueprint: The Six Components

Every AI acquisition system needs six components working together:

<SlideNavigation>
<Slide title="1. Channel Strategy">

**What it is:** Your prioritized list of acquisition channels, mapped to your ICP, budget, and time constraints.

**What you decide:**
- Primary channel (where 60% of effort goes)
- Secondary channel (30%)
- Experimental channel (10%)
- Tools per channel
- Success metrics per channel

**Example (B2B SaaS):**
- Primary: Cold email (Apollo + Instantly)
- Secondary: LinkedIn outreach (Sales Nav + AI personalization)
- Experimental: Website chatbot (Chatbase)

**Example (Creator/Coach):**
- Primary: Content + DM (ConvertKit + ChatGPT)
- Secondary: Community engagement (Reddit/Slack mining)
- Experimental: Podcast guest outreach

</Slide>

<Slide title="2. Data Flow Architecture">

**What it is:** How prospects move from discovery → enrichment → scoring → CRM → sequences → conversations.

**What you map:**
- Source (where leads come from)
- Enrichment (what data gets added)
- Scoring (how they're prioritized)
- Routing (which sequence/human gets them)
- Tracking (how you measure)

**Key decision:** Do you use Zapier/Make to connect tools, or manual CSV exports?

**Budget reality:** Zapier Free (100 tasks/month) works for &lt;50 leads/week. At scale, upgrade to $19.99/mo (750 tasks).

</Slide>

<Slide title="3. Personalization Tiers">

**What it is:** Your three-tier review system from Lesson 3, now with specific volume targets.

**Tier 1 (Top 20%):** Full manual research + custom outreach
- Time: 20-30 min per prospect
- Volume: 10-15/week
- Reply rate target: 25-40%

**Tier 2 (Middle 50%):** AI draft + quick human review
- Time: 5 min per prospect
- Volume: 50-75/week
- Reply rate target: 10-20%

**Tier 3 (Bottom 30%):** Template + segment personalization
- Time: 1 min per prospect
- Volume: 100-150/week
- Reply rate target: 3-8%

</Slide>

<Slide title="4. Automation Boundaries">

**What it is:** Your Automation Failure Matrix from Lesson 6, now with specific "human gate" rules.

**Q1 (Automate Now):**
- Email verification
- CRM data entry
- Sequence triggers
- Warmup rotation

**Q2 (Automate + Human Gate):**
- AI personalization (human reviews top 20%)
- Lead scoring (human overrides edge cases)
- Chatbot qualification (human takes over at buying signal)

**Q3 (Keep Human):**
- Discovery calls
- Pricing/negotiation
- Relationship repair
- Top-tier outreach (Tier 1)

**Q4 (Eliminate):**
- Manual reporting you don't act on
- Perfectionist formatting
- "Spray and pray" sequences

</Slide>

<Slide title="5. Weekly Rhythm">

**What it is:** Your time-blocked calendar from Lesson 7, now with specific outputs per block.

**Monday (90 min):** AI-assisted research + list building
- Output: 50 scored prospects in CRM

**Tuesday (60 min):** AI draft personalization + review + load sequences
- Output: 150 emails queued

**Wednesday (45 min):** Follow-up + chatbot monitoring + LinkedIn engagement
- Output: 20 replies processed, 10 LinkedIn connections

**Thursday (90 min):** Discovery calls + warm outreach
- Output: 3-5 conversations

**Friday (45 min):** Metrics review + strategy tweaks + CRM cleanup
- Output: Updated dashboard, next week's priorities

**Total:** 5.5 hours/week

</Slide>

<Slide title="6. Success Metrics">

**What it is:** Your 5-metric dashboard that tells you if the system is working.

**The 5 Metrics:**
1. **Prospects Added/Week** (target: 20-50)
2. **Emails Sent/Week** (target: 150-500)
3. **Reply Rate** (target: 5-15%)
4. **Conversations Booked/Week** (target: 2-5)
5. **Pipeline Value/Month** (target: 3-5x your monthly revenue goal)

**Leading vs Lagging:**
- Leading (control): Prospects added, emails sent
- Lagging (outcome): Reply rate, conversations, pipeline

**Review cadence:** Weekly for leading, monthly for lagging.

</Slide>
</SlideNavigation>

## The Implementation Sprint: Your First 14 Days

You have a blueprint. Now you need a **launch plan**.

Here's your 14-day implementation sprint. This isn't "someday." This is **starting Monday**.

<SlideNavigation>
<Slide title="Days 1-2: Tool Setup">

**Goal:** Get all tools configured and connected.

**Tasks:**
- [ ] Sign up for tools in your stack (Apollo, Instantly, etc.)
- [ ] Configure email domains and DNS (DKIM, SPF, DMARC)
- [ ] Connect tools via Zapier/Make (or plan manual CSV workflow)
- [ ] Set up CRM fields for scoring (Fit, Signal, Friction)
- [ ] Install chatbot on website (if using)

**Time:** 4-6 hours total

**Output:** Tools live and talking to each other

**Common blockers:**
- DNS propagation takes 24-48 hours (start early)
- Zapier learning curve (use templates)
- CRM field setup (keep it simple: just Score 1-10 + Notes)

</Slide>

<Slide title="Days 3-4: First List Build">

**Goal:** Build your first scored list of 50 prospects.

**Tasks:**
- [ ] Run Apollo/LinkedIn search using ICP filters
- [ ] Export 50 prospects
- [ ] Enrich with ChatGPT research (recent posts, trigger events)
- [ ] Score each prospect (Fit + Signal - Friction)
- [ ] Import to CRM with scores

**Time:** 3-4 hours

**Output:** 50 scored prospects in CRM, segmented by tier

**Quality check:**
- Can you explain why each Tier 1 prospect is a good fit?
- Do you have at least one specific signal per Tier 1 prospect?
- Are scores distributed (not all 8-10)?

</Slide>

<Slide title="Days 5-6: Sequence Creation">

**Goal:** Build sequences for Tier 1 and Tier 2.

**Tasks:**
- [ ] Write Tier 1 manual outreach (10-15 custom emails)
- [ ] Create Tier 2 AI-assisted sequence (3-email sequence with variables)
- [ ] Load sequences into Instantly/Smartlead
- [ ] Set up follow-up triggers (no reply after 3 days → next email)
- [ ] Configure send limits (start conservative: 50/day max)

**Time:** 3-4 hours

**Output:** 2 sequences loaded and ready to launch

**Safety checks:**
- [ ] Personalization passes FASP test (Factual, Actually relevant, Specific, Proud)
- [ ] No more than 3 emails per sequence
- [ ] Unsubscribe link in every email
- [ ] Send volume under 100/day for new domains

</Slide>

<Slide title="Day 7: Launch + Monitor">

**Goal:** Send first batch and monitor deliverability.

**Tasks:**
- [ ] Send Tier 1 emails manually (10-15 emails)
- [ ] Launch Tier 2 sequence (50 emails)
- [ ] Monitor inbox placement (use mail-tester.com)
- [ ] Track opens/replies in real-time
- [ ] Respond to any replies within 2 hours

**Time:** 2-3 hours

**Output:** First campaign live, initial data coming in

**Red flags:**
- Open rate &lt;10% → deliverability issue
- Bounce rate >5% → list quality issue
- Spam complaints >0.1% → messaging issue

</Slide>

<Slide title="Days 8-14: Execute Rhythm">

**Goal:** Run your weekly rhythm for one full week.

**Monday (90 min):** Build next 50-prospect list
**Tuesday (60 min):** Draft + review + queue 150 emails
**Wednesday (45 min):** Process replies, LinkedIn engagement
**Thursday (90 min):** Discovery calls (if any booked)
**Friday (45 min):** Review metrics, adjust strategy

**Time:** 5.5 hours

**Output:** One complete week of data

**Key metrics to track:**
- Prospects added: ___
- Emails sent: ___
- Reply rate: ___%
- Conversations booked: ___
- Pipeline added: $___

</Slide>
</SlideNavigation>

## Pipeline Math: Working Backwards from Your Goal

Let's make sure your system is **sized correctly** for your revenue goal.

Here's the formula:

**Revenue Goal → Deals Needed → Conversations Needed → Replies Needed → Emails Needed**

  title="Pipeline Calculator"
  persistKey="ai-acquisition-strategy-L10-pipeline"

    { id: "emailsPerWeek", label: "Emails Needed/Week", formula: "((((revenue / dealSize) / (closeRate / 100)) / (replyToConvo / 100)) / (replyRate / 100)) / 4", unit: "", precision: 0 }
  ]}
  insight="At {emailsPerWeek} emails/week, you need to add {emailsPerWeek / 3} new prospects weekly (assuming 3-email sequences). That's {emailsPerWeek / 3 / 5} prospects per weekday."
/>

**Example:**
- Revenue goal: $10K/month
- Deal size: $2,500
- Close rate: 20%
- Reply → Conversation: 10%
- Reply rate: 8%

**Math:**
- Deals needed: $10K / $2,500 = **4 deals/month**
- Conversations: 4 / 20% = **20 conversations/month**
- Replies: 20 / 10% = **200 replies/month**
- Emails: 200 / 8% = **2,500 emails/month** = **625/week**

**Reality check:** At 625 emails/week, you need ~200 new prospects/week (assuming 3-email sequences). That's **40 prospects/day** (M-F).

**Is that realistic?** With AI-assisted research (5 min/prospect for Tier 2), that's 200 min = **3.3 hours/week just on research**. Add drafting, reviewing, sending = **5-6 hours/week total**. ✅ Fits your time budget.

<InsightCard icon="⚠️" title="When the Math Doesn't Work">
If your pipeline math requires >10 hours/week or >$300/month in tools, you have three options:

1. **Increase reply rate** (better targeting, better personalization)
2. **Increase close rate** (better discovery, better qualification)
3. **Increase deal size** (move upmarket, add services)

Don't just "send more emails." That's the trap.
</InsightCard>

## The 90-Day Calibration Plan

Your system won't be perfect on Day 1. That's expected. Here's how to **calibrate** over 90 days.

<ProgressiveReveal title="90-Day Calibration Roadmap" persistKey="ai-acquisition-strategy-L10-calibration">

<RevealSection title="Days 1-30: Establish Baseline">

**Goal:** Get clean data on what's working.

**Focus:**
- Run your rhythm consistently (5.5 hours/week)
- Don't change too many variables at once
- Track all 5 metrics weekly
- Identify your biggest bottleneck

**Common bottlenecks:**
- **Low prospect volume** → Spend more time on discovery (Monday block)
- **Low reply rate** → Improve personalization (Tier 2 quality)
- **Low conversation rate** → Improve qualification (scoring model)
- **Low close rate** → Improve discovery calls (Track 2 skills)

**End of Month 1 Review:**
- [ ] Which metric is furthest from target?
- [ ] What's the root cause? (Targeting? Messaging? Timing?)
- [ ] What's the one change to test in Month 2?

</RevealSection>

<RevealSection title="Days 31-60: Optimize Bottleneck">

**Goal:** Fix the biggest bottleneck from Month 1.

**If bottleneck was prospect volume:**
- Expand discovery sources (add Reddit, podcasts, communities)
- Increase Monday block to 120 min
- Use AI to speed up research (ChatGPT batch prompts)

**If bottleneck was reply rate:**
- Improve Tier 2 personalization (add one more research signal)
- Test new subject lines (A/B test)
- Segment further (split broad ICP into 2-3 sub-segments)

**If bottleneck was conversation rate:**
- Tighten scoring model (raise threshold for Tier 1)
- Improve qualification questions in chatbot
- Add "intent signal" requirement (recent post, job change, funding)

**If bottleneck was close rate:**
- Review discovery call recordings (if any)
- Revisit Track 2 (Sales Psychology) lessons
- Add "qualification checklist" before booking calls

**End of Month 2 Review:**
- [ ] Did the bottleneck improve?
- [ ] What's the new bottleneck?
- [ ] Are you hitting 3 out of 5 metric targets?

</RevealSection>

<RevealSection title="Days 61-90: Scale What Works">

**Goal:** Double down on what's working, cut what's not.

**If a channel is working:**
- Increase time allocation (e.g., 70% primary, 20% secondary, 10% experimental)
- Increase tool budget if ROI is clear (e.g., upgrade Apollo to Pro)
- Document the playbook (what works, what doesn't)

**If a channel isn't working:**
- Cut it (move time to what's working)
- Or pivot (e.g., cold email → warm intros, LinkedIn DMs → LinkedIn content)

**If you're hitting all 5 targets:**
- Increase volume targets by 25%
- Test a new experimental channel
- Consider hiring help (VA for research, SDR for follow-up)

**End of Month 3 Review:**
- [ ] Are you consistently hitting 4-5 out of 5 targets?
- [ ] Is the system running on ≤7 hours/week?
- [ ] Is the cost still under $200/month?
- [ ] Do you have a documented playbook you could hand to someone else?

If yes to all four → **You have a working AI acquisition system.** 🎉

</RevealSection>

</ProgressiveReveal>
