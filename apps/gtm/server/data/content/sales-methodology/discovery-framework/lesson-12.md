# AI Roleplay: Discovery Practice

You have mastered the **Psychology** of DISC and the **Structure** of BANT/MEDDIC. But as the saying goes: _"Everyone has a plan until they get punched in the mouth."_

In sales, the "punch" is the guarded prospect, the "happy ears" distraction, or the "no decision" stall. To succeed as a solo founder, you must build **Muscle Memory**. (2025 State of Sales). This lesson is dedicated to structured AI Roleplay simulations, using the **SoloFrame Hub 3D Matrix**.

---

## 1. Matching Discovery to Deal Size (The Velocity Filter)

Before you roleplay, internalize the **Rabbit, Deer, Elephant** framework to scale your effort. (2026 Acquisition Trends).

| Deal Tier    | ACV Range  | Discovery Framework    | Optimal Duration |
| :----------- | :--------- | :--------------------- | :--------------- |
| **Rabbit**   | &lt;$5k    | Lightweight BANT (MVQ) | 12-18 Minutes    |
| **Deer**     | $5k - $50k | BANT + Metrics (ROI)   | 18-35 Minutes    |
| **Elephant** | $50k+      | Full MEDDIC            | 45-89 Minutes    |

**The Solo Rule:** Discovery length must correlate with deal size. Using Elephant-level discovery on a Rabbit-sized deal is "founder suicide" by overhead. (2025 State of Buyer Behavior).

---

## 2. Roleplay Drill 1: The "Why Now?" Diagnostic

**Scenario:** An Inbound lead arrives via your website. They want "just a demo."

- **Persona:** High-D (Results-Oriented).
- **The Goal:** Use "Intent Throttling" to move back into discovery.
- **The Key Question:** _"I'm happy to show you the dashboard, but you've had this problem for 6 months. Why did today become the day it moved to the top of your list?"_

## 3. Roleplay Drill 2: The "Shadow Committee" Map

**Scenario:** You are speaking to a Head of Engineering (High-C) about a $30k deal.

- **The Goal:** Uncover the **Economic Buyer** and the **Paper Process**.
- **The Key Script:** _"Walk me through exactly whose desk the contract sits on after you hit 'approve'. Is there a CFO or Procurement lead we should get ahead of today?"_

## 4. Roleplay Drill 3: The "Level 3 Pain" Funnel

**Scenario:** A prospect says their conversion rates are "okay, but could be better."

- **The Goal:** Move from Level 1 (Tactical) to Level 3 (Personal) pain.
- **The Key Script:** _"If conversion stays at 'okay' for another quarter, how does that affect your standing with the VP or your team's launch targets for Q1?"_

---

## 5. Performance Metrics (DASH Score)

In your AI roleplay sessions, focus on these 2026 Benchmarks:

1.  **Talk-to-Listen Ratio:** Aim for **54-58% talk time**. (2025 Benchmarks). You are the expert diagnostician; your job is to lead, summarizing the pain and prescriptive value.
2.  **Strategic Intent:** Did you ask "Why Now?" within the first 10 minutes?
3.  **Linguistic Interface:** Did you match the prospect's DISC pace (Motor) and focus (Compass)?
