## Step 1: Create Your Account

Sign up at the main site and complete the registration form. You'll need:

- An email address
- A password (minimum 8 characters)

After signing up, you'll be taken to the onboarding flow automatically.

## Step 2: Complete Your Profile

Your profile powers the AI coaching system. The more context you provide, the better your coaching experience will be.

**Required fields:**

- Your name
- Business name and description
- Your role (solo founder, co-founder, consultant)
- Business stage (idea, pre-revenue, early revenue, growth)

**Optional but recommended:**

- Target customer description
- Current revenue range
- Biggest GTM challenge

<Banner type="important">
  Your business context is used **only** to personalize AI coaching responses.
  It is never shared with other users or used for marketing.
</Banner>

## Step 3: Take the Readiness Score

The Readiness Score is a 5-minute diagnostic that evaluates your GTM readiness across 8 dimensions:

1. **Positioning Clarity** — How well you articulate your value
2. **ICP Definition** — How precisely you've defined your target customer
3. **Lead Generation** — Your ability to create pipeline
4. **Sales Process** — Whether you have a repeatable sales approach
5. **Content & Marketing** — Your marketing engine maturity
6. **Customer Success** — Post-sale retention and expansion
7. **Systems & Operations** — Automation and measurement
8. **Mindset & Resilience** — Your founder mental fitness

Each dimension is scored 1-10, giving you a clear picture of where to focus.

## Step 4: Review Your Learning Path

Based on your Readiness Score, the system generates a personalized learning path. This isn't a rigid sequence — you can always explore any track — but it prioritizes the areas where you'll see the fastest impact.

Your dashboard will show:

- **Recommended next lesson**
- **Track progress** across all 8 tracks
- **Weekly goals** based on your pace setting

## Step 5: Start Your First Lesson

Each lesson follows a consistent structure:

1. **Concept** — The key idea explained clearly (5-7 min read)
2. **Exercise** — An interactive component where you apply the concept
3. **AI Coach** — Ask questions and get personalized feedback
4. **Quiz** — Quick check to reinforce learning

<Banner type="note">
  You don't have to complete exercises in one sitting. Your progress is saved
  automatically and persists across sessions.
</Banner>

## Step 6: Join a Pod (Optional)

After completing your first few lessons, you'll be invited to join an accountability pod. Pods are small groups of 4-6 founders matched by:

- Business stage
- Industry focus
- Communication style (via DISC assessment)
- Timezone compatibility

Pod activities include weekly check-ins, win sharing, and peer feedback in the community forum.
