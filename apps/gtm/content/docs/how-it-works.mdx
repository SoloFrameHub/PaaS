## Learning Structure

The Academy is organized into a clear hierarchy:

```
Tracks (8)
  └── Courses (2-5 per track)
       └── Lessons (8-10 per course)
            ├── Content (concept + examples)
            ├── Interactive Exercise
            ├── AI Coach
            └── Quiz
```

Each level builds on the previous one, but you're free to explore non-linearly based on your needs.

## Lesson Anatomy

Every lesson follows a consistent four-part structure:

### 1. Concept (5-7 minutes)

The core idea explained with real-world examples. Lessons use practical, founder-focused language — no academic theory. Each concept is actionable and can be applied immediately.

### 2. Interactive Exercise

Hands-on components built into the lesson:

- **Range Sliders** — Self-assess your confidence or current state
- **Checklists** — Track completion of multi-step activities
- **Workshops** — Guided templates like ICP Builder or Persona Builder
- **Assessment Engines** — AI-evaluated exercises that give scored feedback

All interactive state is automatically saved. You can leave and return without losing progress.

### 3. AI Coach

A contextual AI coach embedded in each lesson. Unlike generic chatbots, the coach:

- Knows your business context (from your profile)
- Understands the specific lesson topic
- References the curriculum content in responses
- Gives actionable, specific advice — not platitudes

Ask the coach anything related to the lesson topic and your business. Common questions:

- "How does this apply to my B2B SaaS targeting HR teams?"
- "Can you give me an example email using this framework?"
- "What's the most common mistake founders make here?"

### 4. Quiz

A short quiz (3-5 questions) that reinforces the lesson's key concepts. Quizzes are not graded — they're learning tools. You can retake them anytime.

## Progress Tracking

Your progress is tracked at multiple levels:

- **Lesson completion** — Marked when you've viewed the content
- **Exercise completion** — Tracked per interactive component
- **Quiz scores** — Best score recorded
- **Track progress** — Percentage across all lessons in a track
- **Streaks** — Consecutive days of engagement

The dashboard gives you a clear view of where you are and what to do next.

## Assessments

At key milestones, you'll encounter AI-powered assessments. These are different from quizzes — they evaluate open-ended responses using AI:

1. You submit a written response (e.g., your positioning statement)
2. AI evaluates it against a rubric specific to the exercise
3. You receive a score and detailed feedback
4. You can revise and resubmit to improve your score

Assessment scores contribute to your overall Readiness Score, which evolves as you progress.

<Banner type="note">
  Assessment evaluations use a 60-second processing window to ensure thorough
  feedback. If you see a loading indicator, it's working through your response —
  don't refresh.
</Banner>

## Recommended Pace

Most founders complete a lesson in 15-30 minutes. We recommend:

- **Minimum**: 2 lessons per week
- **Recommended**: 3-4 lessons per week
- **Intensive**: 1 lesson per day

The system adapts to your pace and sends gentle reminders if you haven't engaged recently. You can adjust notification frequency in Settings.
