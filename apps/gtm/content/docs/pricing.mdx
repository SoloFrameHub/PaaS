## Current Pricing

Solo GTM OS is currently in **beta**. During the beta period, access is free for early members.

### Post-Beta Pricing

After beta, the platform will offer:

- **Monthly**: $29.95/month
- **Annual**: $259.95/year (save ~28%)

Both plans include:

- Full access to all 8 Academy tracks (80+ lessons)
- AI coaching in every lesson
- Roleplay practice with AI personas
- Community forum and pod membership
- The 8 GTM Frameworks book
- Readiness Score and assessments
- All templates and tools
- Weekly AI-facilitated pod sessions

### Free Trial

New members receive a **7-day free trial** with full platform access. No credit card required to start.

### Beta Members

If you joined during the beta period, you'll receive advance notice and special pricing when the paid plans launch. Beta members always get the best available rate.

## Managing Your Subscription

### Billing Portal

Access your billing details from **Settings → Billing**. From there you can:

- View your current plan
- Update payment method
- Download invoices
- Switch between monthly and annual billing
- Cancel your subscription

### Payments

Payments are processed through **Polar.sh**, a payment platform built for developer tools and SaaS. Polar supports:

- Credit and debit cards
- PayPal (where available)
- Regional payment methods

### Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time. When you cancel:

- Your access continues until the end of your current billing period
- Your progress, notes, and assessment results are preserved
- You can reactivate at any time to pick up where you left off
- Pod membership is paused (you'll be re-matched if you return)

<Banner type="note">
  We don't believe in making cancellation difficult. If the platform isn't
  providing value, you should be able to leave easily. We'd rather earn your
  subscription every month.
</Banner>

## Refunds

If you're unsatisfied within the first 30 days of a paid subscription, contact support for a full refund. No questions asked.
