## What is Solo GTM OS?

Solo GTM OS is not a course — it's an **operating system for customer acquisition**. Built specifically for solo founders, it combines AI-powered coaching, structured frameworks, and community accountability to replace what would otherwise cost $5K+/month in sales consultants.

Think of it as your personal GTM (Go-To-Market) co-pilot that adapts to your business, your pace, and your style.

## Who Is This For?

Solo GTM OS is designed for:

- **Solo SaaS founders** who need to acquire their first 10-100 customers
- **Bootstrapped builders** who can't afford a sales team but need systematic revenue
- **Technical founders** who are great at building but struggle with selling
- **Consultants and freelancers** transitioning to productized services or SaaS

<Banner type="note">
  You don't need sales experience. The system is designed to take you from "I
  don't know where to start" to "I have a repeatable acquisition process."
</Banner>

## Core Pillars

### 1. Structured Academy

Eight learning tracks covering every phase of customer acquisition — from positioning and ICP definition through lead generation, sales conversations, and customer success. Each track contains 8-10 lessons with interactive exercises.

### 2. AI Coaching

Every lesson includes an AI coach that understands your specific business context. Ask questions, get feedback on your work, and receive personalized guidance — not generic advice.

### 3. Roleplay Practice

Practice real sales scenarios with AI personas before you face actual prospects. The system simulates skeptical buyers, technical evaluators, and budget-conscious decision makers.

### 4. Community Pods

Small accountability groups of 4-6 founders matched by business stage and goals. Weekly check-ins, shared wins, and peer feedback keep you moving forward.

### 5. Readiness Score

A diagnostic assessment that identifies your specific gaps across 8 GTM dimensions. Your personalized learning path is generated based on where you actually need help.

## How It Works

1. **Take the Readiness Score** — A 5-minute assessment identifies your strengths and gaps
2. **Get Your Path** — AI generates a personalized curriculum based on your results
3. **Learn by Doing** — Each lesson includes interactive exercises, not just reading
4. **Practice with AI** — Roleplay scenarios build real skills before you talk to prospects
5. **Stay Accountable** — Your pod keeps you on track with weekly rhythm
6. **Track Progress** — Dashboard shows completion, streaks, and skill development
