## Built-in Tools

Solo GTM OS includes interactive tools that go beyond static templates. These are embedded directly in lessons and available from your dashboard.

### ICP Workshop

A guided, multi-step workshop that helps you define your Ideal Customer Profile. Includes:

- Industry and company size targeting
- Decision maker persona mapping
- Pain point prioritization
- Buying trigger identification
- Scoring criteria definition

Your ICP workshop results persist and can be updated as you learn more about your market.

### Persona Builder

Create detailed buyer personas with AI assistance:

- Demographics and psychographics
- Day-in-the-life scenarios
- Goals, frustrations, and motivations
- Information sources and buying behavior
- Objection patterns

### Assessment Engine

AI-powered evaluation of your work:

- Submit positioning statements, cold emails, or sales scripts
- Receive scored feedback against lesson-specific rubrics
- Track improvement over time
- Compare against model responses

### Roleplay Simulator

Practice sales conversations with AI personas:

- Choose your scenario (discovery call, objection handling, closing)
- Select a persona type (skeptic, builder, perfectionist, mentor)
- Run through realistic multi-turn conversations
- Receive a debrief with strengths and areas to improve

## Interactive Lesson Components

Throughout the curriculum, you'll encounter these interactive elements:

### Range Sliders

Self-assessment tools that help you gauge your current confidence or capability in specific areas. Your responses are saved and tracked over time to show growth.

### Interactive Checklists

Step-by-step activity trackers for multi-part exercises. Check off items as you complete them — progress is saved automatically.

### Quizzes

Knowledge checks embedded in lessons. Results are saved but not graded — they're learning reinforcement tools.

<Banner type="note">
  All interactive component state is saved using your session. You can leave a
  lesson mid-exercise and return later without losing progress. Look for the
  auto-save indicator.
</Banner>
