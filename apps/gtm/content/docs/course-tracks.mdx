## Track Overview

The Academy contains 8 tracks organized from foundational to advanced. Each track addresses a distinct phase of the customer acquisition journey.

## Foundations

**Focus**: Positioning, ICP definition, and value proposition

This is where most founders should start. Before you can sell anything, you need crystal clarity on:

- Who your ideal customer is (and isn't)
- What problem you solve and why it matters now
- How to articulate your value in a way that resonates
- How to choose your initial go-to-market path

**Courses include:**

- Choose Your Path
- ICP Builder
- Positioning & Value
- List Building Fundamentals
- Sales Psychology

<Banner type="important">
  If your Readiness Score shows low Positioning Clarity or ICP Definition, start
  here regardless of your experience level. Everything downstream depends on
  these foundations.
</Banner>

## Lead Generation

**Focus**: Building pipeline through outbound and inbound channels

Learn systematic approaches to generating qualified leads:

- Cold email that actually gets replies
- LinkedIn outreach frameworks
- Inbound content strategies
- Lead magnets and nurture sequences
- Referral and partner channels

## Sales Methodology

**Focus**: Turning conversations into revenue

Practice-heavy track focused on the actual selling process:

- Discovery call frameworks
- Objection handling playbooks
- Pricing and packaging conversations
- Proposal and closing techniques
- Follow-up systems

This track makes heavy use of the **Roleplay** feature — you'll practice conversations with AI personas before talking to real prospects.

## Marketing Engine

**Focus**: Content, SEO, and social proof

Build a sustainable marketing engine that generates inbound interest:

- Content strategy for solo founders
- SEO fundamentals that compound
- Social proof and case study creation
- Newsletter and email marketing
- Community-led growth

## AI Acquisition

**Focus**: Leveraging AI for prospecting and outreach

Modern approaches to customer acquisition using AI tools:

- AI-powered prospect research
- Automated outreach personalization
- Using AI for competitive analysis
- Building AI-enhanced workflows
- Scaling with AI agents

## Creator Track

**Focus**: Audience building and community-led sales

For founders who want to build an audience as their acquisition channel:

- Audience-to-buyer conversion
- Community-led sales
- Creator metrics that matter
- DM selling and social commerce
- Webinar and challenge funnels
- Scaling creator-led sales

## Customer Success

**Focus**: Onboarding, retention, and expansion revenue

Revenue doesn't end at the sale. This track covers:

- Customer onboarding systems
- Retention metrics and red flags
- Expansion and upsell strategies
- Building customer advocacy
- Reducing churn systematically

## Operations & Systems

**Focus**: Scaling, automation, and measurement

Turn ad-hoc activities into repeatable systems:

- CRM setup and pipeline management
- Metrics dashboards and KPIs
- Automation workflows
- Hiring your first sales help
- Scaling from founder-led to team-led sales

## Choosing Your Starting Track

Your Readiness Score recommends a starting point, but here's a general guide:

| Your Situation                   | Start With                    |
| -------------------------------- | ----------------------------- |
| Pre-product or just launched     | Foundations                   |
| Have a product, no customers yet | Foundations → Lead Generation |
| Some customers, want more        | Sales Methodology             |
| Growing but no system            | Operations & Systems          |
| Want to build an audience        | Creator Track                 |
| Want to leverage AI tools        | AI Acquisition                |
