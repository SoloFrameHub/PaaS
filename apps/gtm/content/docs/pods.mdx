## Accountability Pods

Pods are small groups of 4-6 founders matched by the system to maximize mutual support. They're the accountability backbone of Solo GTM OS.

### How Matching Works

The matching algorithm considers:

- **Business stage** — Pre-revenue founders are matched with other pre-revenue founders
- **Industry focus** — Similar or complementary industries when possible
- **Communication style** — DISC profiles for compatible group dynamics
- **Timezone** — Overlapping windows for synchronous interaction
- **Goals** — Aligned near-term objectives

### Pod Activities

Each pod follows a weekly rhythm facilitated by AI:

**Monday: Week Kick-off**

- AI facilitator posts the weekly prompt
- Members share their top goals for the week
- Accountability commitments are set

**Wednesday: Mid-week Check-in**

- Progress updates from each member
- Blockers and questions shared
- Peer feedback and encouragement

**Friday: Win Sharing**

- Celebrate completed milestones
- Share lessons learned
- Set weekend reflection prompts

### Pod Health

The system monitors pod health using several signals:

- **Participation rate** — Are all members engaging?
- **Response time** — How quickly do members respond to each other?
- **Activity diversity** — Are members doing more than just check-ins?
- **Sentiment** — Is the tone supportive and constructive?

If a pod's health score drops, the AI facilitator adjusts its approach — sending additional prompts, highlighting wins, or suggesting discussion topics.

<Banner type="note">
  Pod participation is optional but strongly recommended. Data shows that
  members in active pods complete 3x more lessons and retain 2x longer than solo
  learners.
</Banner>

### Leaving or Switching Pods

You can leave your current pod at any time from the Community settings. If you'd like to be re-matched with a different group, request a pod transfer and the system will find a better fit.

## Milestones & Celebrations

The system automatically recognizes achievements:

- **First lesson completed** — Welcome to the journey
- **Track completion** — You've mastered a full track
- **Streak milestones** — 7-day, 30-day, 60-day learning streaks
- **Assessment excellence** — Scored 8+ on an assessment
- **Community contribution** — Helped a pod member with useful feedback

Milestones are shared in your pod and appear on your profile. They're designed to maintain momentum without being gimmicky.
