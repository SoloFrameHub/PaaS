## The Readiness Score

The Readiness Score is your GTM diagnostic. It evaluates your current state across 8 dimensions, each scored 1-10:

1. **Positioning Clarity** — Can you articulate your value proposition clearly?
2. **ICP Definition** — Do you know exactly who you're selling to?
3. **Lead Generation** — Can you consistently create new pipeline?
4. **Sales Process** — Do you have a repeatable approach to closing?
5. **Content & Marketing** — Is your marketing generating awareness?
6. **Customer Success** — Are you retaining and growing existing customers?
7. **Systems & Operations** — Are your processes automated and measured?
8. **Mindset & Resilience** — How's your founder mental fitness?

### Taking the Assessment

The Readiness Score takes approximately 5 minutes. For each dimension, you'll answer 3-4 questions that probe your current capabilities. Answers are evaluated by AI against a calibrated rubric.

### Understanding Your Results

After completing the assessment, you'll see:

- **Radar chart** showing all 8 dimensions
- **Overall score** (average across dimensions)
- **Personalized recommendations** for where to start
- **Comparison** to benchmarks by business stage

<Banner type="note">
  Your Readiness Score is private. It's never shared with other members or
  displayed publicly. It exists solely to guide your learning path.
</Banner>

### Reassessment

You can retake the Readiness Score at any time to track your progress. We recommend reassessing every 30 days. The system tracks your score history so you can see improvement over time.

## In-Lesson Assessments

Beyond the Readiness Score, individual lessons contain assessments — open-ended exercises evaluated by AI:

### How Assessment Grading Works

1. **You complete an exercise** — Write a positioning statement, draft a cold email, etc.
2. **AI evaluates your submission** — Using lesson-specific rubric criteria
3. **You receive structured feedback:**
   - Numerical score
   - What you did well
   - Specific areas for improvement
   - Model response for comparison
4. **Optional revision** — Improve and resubmit for a higher score

### Assessment Types

- **Written responses** — Draft copy, scripts, strategies
- **Framework application** — Apply taught frameworks to your business
- **Self-evaluation** — Rate yourself on specific criteria with AI validation
- **Scenario analysis** — Analyze a hypothetical situation and recommend actions

### Scoring

Assessments use a 1-10 scale with clear rubric definitions at each level. A score of 7+ indicates strong competence. Scores are stored on your profile and contribute to your evolving Readiness Score.
