## Getting Started

<Accordion title="Do I need sales experience to use Solo GTM OS?" active>
  No. The platform is designed for founders with zero sales background. The
  Foundations track starts from first principles — defining your customer,
  articulating your value, and building a systematic approach from scratch.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How long does it take to complete the full curriculum?">
  At the recommended pace of 3-4 lessons per week, the full curriculum takes
  approximately 5-6 months. However, most founders see results after the first
  2-3 weeks by applying what they learn immediately. You don't need to complete
  everything before taking action.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I skip tracks or lessons?">
  Yes. While we recommend following the Readiness Score's suggested path, you
  can access any lesson in any order. The curriculum is designed to be useful
  both sequentially and as a reference library.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is the content available in Spanish?">
  Yes. We are actively translating the curriculum to Spanish. Many tracks are
  already available in Spanish, with full coverage planned. You can switch
  languages from your profile settings.
</Accordion>

## AI & Coaching

<Accordion title="How is AI coaching different from ChatGPT?">
  Three key differences: (1) The coach knows your specific business context from
  your profile. (2) It's trained on the curriculum content via RAG, so it gives
  advice grounded in proven frameworks, not generic responses. (3) It
  understands where you are in the learning journey and tailors its guidance
  accordingly.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is my business data used to train AI models?">
  No. Your business context is sent to AI providers (OpenAI, Anthropic) to
  generate coaching responses, but it is not used for model training. These
  providers have explicit data processing agreements that prohibit training on
  customer data.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I use my own AI API keys?">
  The platform uses its own API keys by default. Custom API key support may be
  added in a future release based on demand.
</Accordion>

## Community

<Accordion title="What if my pod isn't a good fit?">
  You can request a pod transfer at any time from Community settings. The system
  will re-match you based on updated criteria. You can also leave pods
  temporarily if you need a break.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Are the AI personas real people?">
  No. AI personas (Alex, Jordan, Morgan, Sam) are clearly marked AI participants
  in the forum. They're designed to stimulate discussion and provide diverse
  perspectives, not to impersonate humans. Their responses are AI-generated.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I invite someone to my pod?">
  Pods are system-matched to ensure balanced dynamics. However, you can invite
  colleagues to Solo GTM OS, and the matching algorithm will consider placing
  you in the same pod if your profiles are compatible.
</Accordion>

## Billing & Account

<Accordion title="Is there a free tier?">
  During beta, the platform is free. Post-beta, there will be a 7-day free
  trial. We're evaluating a permanent free tier with limited access for future
  launch.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I get a refund?">
  Yes. Within the first 30 days of a paid subscription, you can request a full
  refund from support with no questions asked.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens to my data if I cancel?">
  Your progress, notes, assessment results, and forum posts are preserved. If
  you reactivate, everything will be exactly where you left it. We retain your
  data for 12 months after cancellation. After that, it's permanently deleted
  unless you request earlier deletion.
</Accordion>

## Technical

<Accordion title="Does Solo GTM OS work on mobile?">
  Yes. The platform is fully responsive and works on mobile browsers. A
  progressive web app (PWA) is available — you can add it to your home screen
  for an app-like experience.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What browsers are supported?">
  All modern browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge. We recommend the latest
  version for the best experience.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is there an API?">
  Not currently. The platform is designed for direct use. If you have an
  integration need, reach out to support and we'll evaluate it.
</Accordion>
