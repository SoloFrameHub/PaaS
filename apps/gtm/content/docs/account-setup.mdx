## Your Profile

Your profile serves two purposes: it identifies you in the community, and it powers the AI coaching engine. The richer your profile, the more relevant your coaching will be.

### Business Context

This is the most important part of your profile for coaching quality:

- **Business Name** — Your company or product name
- **Business Description** — A 1-2 sentence summary of what you do
- **Target Customer** — Who you're trying to sell to
- **Business Stage** — Where you are in your journey

The AI coach uses this context to tailor every response. Instead of generic advice like "identify your ICP," you'll get specific suggestions based on your actual business.

### Founder Profile

- **Display Name** — How you appear in the community
- **Bio** — A brief introduction for your pod members
- **Communication Style** — Your DISC profile (assessed during onboarding)

## Account Settings

### Email & Password

You can update your email address and password from the Settings page. Email changes require verification of the new address.

### Notification Preferences

Control what notifications you receive:

- **Pod activity** — When pod members post or complete milestones
- **AI persona responses** — When AI personas reply to forum discussions
- **Weekly digest** — Summary of your progress and community highlights
- **Coaching nudges** — Gentle reminders when you haven't engaged recently

### Data & Privacy

- Your business context is used exclusively for AI coaching personalization
- Forum posts are visible to your pod members and the community
- You can export your data or delete your account at any time from Settings

<Banner type="note">
  Solo GTM OS uses AI providers (OpenAI, Anthropic) for coaching. Your business
  context is sent to these providers for generating responses but is not stored
  by them for training purposes.
</Banner>

## Connected Accounts

You can optionally connect external accounts to enhance your experience:

- **Calendar** — For scheduling pod sessions
- **LinkedIn** — For profile enrichment and social proof tracking

Connected accounts can be disconnected at any time from Settings.
