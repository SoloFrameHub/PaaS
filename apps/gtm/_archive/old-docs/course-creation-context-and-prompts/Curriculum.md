### Why DISC Moves Before Discovery Framework


The original Course 2 (Discovery Framework) references DISC personalities in its roleplay exercises. Course 3 (DISC) is foundational knowledge required BEFORE practicing discovery. The correct learning sequence is:



1. **ICP** → Know WHO you're targeting (companies + roles)
2. **DISC** → Understand HOW different people communicate
3. **Discovery** → Practice WHAT to ask (using DISC in roleplays)
