## COURSE OVERVIEW

### Course Description

60% of Google searches now end without a click to any website. On mobile, this jumps to 77%. Google's AI Overviews appear on ~13% of queries, reducing organic CTR by 30-47% for links pushed below them. The search landscape has fundamentally shifted: you're no longer optimizing just for humans to visit your site—you're optimizing for AI models to read your site, trust it, and cite it as the answer.

This course teaches both traditional SEO fundamentals AND the emerging Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) tactics that get your content cited by AI assistants like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google's AI Overviews. The goal is visibility in both traditional search AND AI-powered discovery.

For solo founders, this shift is actually an advantage. You can't outspend VC-funded competitors on ads, and you can't out-write content farms on volume. But you CAN out-teach them. AI prioritizes E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness)—exactly what genuine practitioners bring.

### Why This Course Matters

**Founder Pain Points Addressed:**
- "My blog posts get no organic traffic"
- "I don't understand technical SEO—it's too complicated"
- "AI is stealing all the traffic—is SEO even worth it?"
- "My competitors rank above me even though my content is better"
- "How do I get ChatGPT to mention my product?"

**The Core Insight:** The goal is no longer just "getting the click" but "getting the citation." You're optimizing for an AI model to read your site, trust it, and cite it as the answer.

### Learning Outcomes

By the end of this course, you will be able to:

1. **Understand the 2025 search landscape** including zero-click searches, AI Overviews, and answer engines
2. **Implement SEO fundamentals** including keyword research, on-page optimization, and technical basics
3. **Structure content for AI citation** using inverted pyramid format and answer-first writing
4. **Implement schema markup** including FAQPage, Product, Course, Person, and Organization schemas
5. **Build entity authority** establishing E-E-A-T signals that make you a trusted source
6. **Optimize for Google AI Overviews** with content structures that get featured
7. **Get cited by ChatGPT and Perplexity** understanding what makes sources citable
8. **Track AI citations** using modern tools to measure "Share of Model"

## SOURCE FRAMEWORKS AND RESEARCH

### From AEO Research Document

**The Zero-Click Reality:**
- 60% of Google searches end without a click (77% on mobile)
- Google AI Overviews appear on ~13% of queries
- AIO reduces organic CTR by **30-47%** for links pushed below

**Dual Context: B2B vs. Creator Entity Strategy:**

| Feature | B2B SaaS Founder | Creator/Coach Founder |
|---------|------------------|----------------------|
| Primary Entity | The Product (Software/Service) | The Person (You) |
| Validation Source | G2, Capterra, Documentation | Social Profiles, Podcasts, Newsletter |
| AEO Goal | "What is the best CRM for solo founders?" | "Who is [Name] and are they legit?" |
| Knowledge Graph | Must claim **Organization** schema | Must claim **Person** schema |

**Case Studies:**
- **Loser (Generic Content):** DIY site lost **70%** traffic—AI summarized tutorials without clicks
- **Winner (Proprietary Data):** Original research sites see *increased* citations—AI can't hallucinate data

**The Inverted Pyramid Content Structure:**
1. **Direct Answer (First 50 Words):** Snippet-ready definition upfront
   - Bad: "In this article, we will explore..."
   - Good: "Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) for solo founders typically ranges from $50–$150..."
2. **Q&A Formatting:** H2 headers as questions, immediate paragraph as answer
3. **Tables:** 28% more likely to be cited than paragraphs

**Tools for Tracking (2025):**
| Tool | Use Case |
|------|----------|
| ZipTie.dev, Keyword.com | Track citations in ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AIO |
| GA4 (Custom Regex) | Catch traffic from perplexity.ai, chatgpt.com |
| Kalicube Pro | Entity/Knowledge Panel management |
