In September 2025, I paused publishing to do something first: prove the frameworks in a real, working system. Instead of adding another book to the stack, I wanted a live environment that shows these ideas working with real founders.

It became obvious that solo founders don’t just need frameworks on paper—they need an implementation system. So I built the Lead Generation & Sales Academy on a serverless, AI‑native stack (Firebase, Genkit, Gemini 2.5 Pro) as a living curriculum that evolves weekly with what actually works.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the weekly time commitment?
Expect to spend about 3–5 hours per week. This includes learning the framework modules (Monday), practicing with the AI (Tue/Wed), and executing real calls (Thursday/Friday).
Do I need a product to join?
Yes. This is an execution academy, not a theoretical one. You need something to sell, even if it's just an MVP or a service, so you can build real artifacts and handle real objections. You can join the founding member waitlist now and be ready with at least a simple offer by the January 21, 2026 beta launch.
Is this just a video course?
No. There is no “sit-and-watch” video course here. The academy is a highly interactive, AI-native execution environment: AI roleplay sessions, artifact builders, and a pipeline state machine that responds to your deals in real time. You learn by doing, not by binge‑watching modules.
What if I'm an introvert/engineer?
You are exactly who this is built for. We treat sales as a system of inputs and outputs, not a charisma contest. You start by practicing with AI buyer personas in a safe, consequence-free environment, then bring those scripts and frameworks into real calls when you're ready.
What is the refund policy?
We offer a “Do The Work” Beta Checkpoint Promise. Starting from the day billing begins, if within 30 days you complete the onboarding, 10 roleplay sessions, and 5 real discovery calls and still don’t feel more confident or better equipped, you can request a 100% refund.
