How Your Mind Shapes Your Reality

Anxiety often starts with a single, automatic thought. Understanding the connection between thoughts, feelings, and actions is the foundation of Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT). When you learn to intervene at any point in this cycle, you can transform your entire experience.

💭 Thoughts

What we think affects how we feel and act

💗 Feelings

How we feel affects what we think and do

⚡ Actions

What we do affects how we think and feel

They're all connected. Change one, and you can change them all.
