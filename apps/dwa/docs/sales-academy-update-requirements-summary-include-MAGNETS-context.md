## MAGNETS FRAMEWORK OVERVIEW (From Book Chapter 6)

The MAGNETS framework is a seven-part customer acquisition system:

- **M** - Market Intelligence & Customer Segmentation (identify "golden segment")
- **A** - Acquisition Channel Architecture (SEO, content, social - AUTOMATED)
- **G** - Growth Engine & Journey Optimization (onboarding - POST-SALE)
- **N** - Nurturing & Retention Systems (long-term relationships - POST-SALE)
- **E** - Expansion & Revenue Maximization (upselling - POST-SALE)
- **T** - Tracking & Performance Analytics (growth dashboard - COMPREHENSIVE)
- **S** - Sales & Direct Outreach Systems (prospecting → closing - DIRECT)

**Key Distinction:**
- Components A, G, N, E = Automated/passive systems (NOT Sales Academy scope)
- Components M (partial), S (complete), T (partial) = Direct sales systems (IS Sales Academy scope)

The book teaches the complete MAGNETS framework. The Sales Academy trains execution of the M-S-T components that technical founders fear most.
