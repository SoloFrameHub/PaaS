## Content Quality Assessment

### Scope Boundaries (Critical Success Factor)

✅ **Clear distinction established in every lesson:**
- Creative self-help for emotional regulation and wellbeing
- Licensed art therapy requires ATR-BC certification (board-certified art therapist)
- Licensed music therapy requires MT-BC certification
- Major trauma processing requires licensed trauma-focused therapy (EMDR, PE, CPT)

✅ **Safety framework consistently applied:**
- Containment protocols for difficult content (time limits, grounding plans, stop rules)
- Red flags for when to seek professional help
- Trauma referral criteria (PTSD, sexual assault, complex trauma, severe dissociation)
- Crisis resources provided (988, Crisis Text Line, RAINN, SAMHSA)

### Evidence-Based Content

✅ **Research grading applied throughout:**
- **STRONG:** Affect labeling (amygdala reduction), expressive writing for mild stress, music therapy (clinical)
- **MODERATE:** Journaling for self-awareness, visual art for regulation, narrative processing
- **EMERGING:** Digital creative tools, AI creative tools, online communities
- **LIMITED/MIXED:** Expressive writing for trauma (trauma-focused therapy substantially more effective)

✅ **Therapeutic parity achieved:**
- Comparable depth to therapeutic school courses
- Evidence citations and meta-analyses referenced
- Clinical frameworks identified (Creative Arts, Expressive Writing, Affect Labeling)
- Professional referral criteria clearly stated
