Frequently Asked Questions

What is the weekly time commitment?
Expect to spend about 3-5 hours per week. This includes watching the framework modules (Monday), practicing with the AI (Tue/Wed), and executing real calls (Thursday/Friday).
Do I need a product to join?
Yes. This is an execution academy, not a theoretical one. You need something to sell, even if it's just an MVP or a service, so you can build real artifacts and handle real objections.
Is this just a video course?
No. While there are video frameworks, the core value is the software platform: the AI Roleplay environment, the Artifact Builders, and the Pipeline State Machine. It's a SaaS + Education hybrid.
What if I'm an introvert/engineer?
You are exactly who this is built for. We treat sales as a system of inputs and outputs, not a charisma contest. You will learn scripts and frameworks that remove the need for "charm."
What is the refund policy?
We offer a "Do The Work" Guarantee. If you complete the onboarding, 10 roleplay sessions, and 5 real calls in 30 days and don't feel more confident, we'll refund you 100%.
