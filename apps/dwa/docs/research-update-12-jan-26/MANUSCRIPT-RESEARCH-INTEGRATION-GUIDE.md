## Voice Reminders for Claude Code

**Mike's voice characteristics:**
- First person, practitioner perspective
- "This is what I learned" not "You should do this"
- Acknowledges failures and lessons
- Direct, no fluff
- Humble but confident
- Anti-guru, anti-hype

**Avoid:**
- Excessive bullet points in prose sections
- "Game-changer," "unleash," "skyrocket," "revolutionary"
- Overpromising or hype language
- Generic advice without specifics
- AI-sounding flourishes

**Citation style:**
- Inline for specific data points: "Research shows a 22-29% improvement in conversion rates when..."
- No academic citation format needed - this is a business book
- Attribute major frameworks: "William Marston's DISC model..."
