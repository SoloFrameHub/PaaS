## The Problem

**You're a technical founder. Building products is your superpower. But selling feels like a different language.**

Technical founders face specific barriers:

### The Illegibility Barrier
- 📊 Sales feels like a "black box" — no clear inputs, no predictable outputs
- 🎯 No systematic process to follow step-by-step
- 📋 What works? What doesn't? It's all guesswork

### The Identity Conflict Barrier  
- 🎭 "I don't want to become a salesperson"
- 😰 Sales advice says "adopt a sales persona" — that feels fake
- 💔 Everything you read sounds like manipulation

**Result?** You avoid sales. Deals slip away. Your amazing product stays hidden.

---

## The Solution

**Sales Academy teaches sales as a systematic process — not a personality transformation.**

We reframe sales for how technical minds work:

| Traditional Sales Thinking | Technical Founder's Sales Academy |
|---------------------------|-----------------------------------|

| "Adopt a sales persona" | **You don't need to change who you are** |
| "Fake it till you make it" | **Systematic frameworks you can follow** |
| "It's about relationships" | **Process engineering for conversations** |

## Core Features

### 🤖 AI Roleplay with DISC Personas
Practice with 4 AI buyer personality types before real calls:
- **D (Dominant)** — Direct, results-focused buyers
- **I (Influential)** — Enthusiastic, relationship-driven buyers  
- **S (Steady)** — Patient, stability-seeking buyers
- **C (Conscientious)** — Analytical, detail-oriented buyers

*Unlimited practice. No embarrassment. Real skill building.*

### 📍 7-Stage Pipeline State Machine
Know exactly where each deal stands with clear progression:

1. **Prospecting** → Building lists, researching
2. **Outreach** → Multi-touch sequences
3. **Responded** → Positive reply, scheduling
4. **Discovery** → Qualification conversations
5. **Proposal** → Demos, value articulation
6. **Negotiation** → Objection handling, closing
7. **Closed** → Won or lost (with learnings)

*Each stage has entry/exit criteria, coaching prompts, and templates.*

### 📝 Versioned Sales Artifacts
Build your personal sales system over time:
- **Ideal Customer Profiles** that sharpen with every conversation
- **Objection handlers** that actually work
- **Demo scripts** tailored to your product
- **Outreach sequences** you can reuse and optimize
