You got it — here's the complete feature map for your platform, clean and straightforward.

***

## Core Platform Features (Already Built or In Progress)

- **Course library** — structured lessons organized by topic (OCD, bipolar, burnout, anger management, etc.)[^1]
- **Free + paid tiers** — freemium access for public users, paid subscriptions for full content[^2]
- **User authentication** — anonymous-safe login, no PII required for general access[^3]
- **Self-assessments** — interactive quizzes and reflection activities embedded in lessons[^4]
- **Progress tracking** — users see what they've completed and where they are in a course[^3]
- **Mobile-responsive UI** — accessible on any device[^3]

***

## Provider Tools (The Clinical Layer)

This is what transforms the platform from an education site into a clinical support system.

**Provider accounts**

- Separate login role for licensed therapists and psychiatrists
- Dashboard showing all their active patients/clients
- Ability to browse and preview all course content before assigning

**Assignment engine**

- Provider selects a course, lesson, or module and assigns it to a specific patient
- Patient sees it appear in a "My Care Plan" section of their account
- Provider can set a due date or leave it open-ended

**Progress visibility**

- Provider sees real-time completion status per patient
- Can see self-assessment scores and reflection responses (with patient consent)
- Flags when a patient hasn't engaged in a set number of days

**Session prep notes**

- After a patient completes a lesson, the provider gets a summary of their responses to use as a session starting point

***

## Group Therapy Mode

- **Cohort creation** — provider creates a group (e.g., "DBT Skills — Spring 2026"), sets a start date and course
- **Group enrollment** — patients join via invite code or direct link
- **Shared progress timeline** — all cohort members move through the same content on the same schedule
- **Discussion prompts** — provider posts discussion questions tied to specific lessons; patients respond privately or to the group
- **Facilitator guide** — downloadable PDF companion for providers running live sessions alongside the online content

***

## Self-Directed Public Track

- **Open enrollment courses** — anyone can buy access and go at their own pace
- **Branching scenarios** — interactive "what would you do?" situations that reinforce skills
- **Reflection journals** — private written entries tied to specific lessons, stored securely
- **Skill-practice exercises** — guided CBT/DBT activities with step-by-step prompts
- **Completion certificates** — auto-generated PDF when a user finishes a course

***

## Certification \& Credentialing

- **CEU-eligible course completion** — partner with a credentialing body so providers earn Continuing Education Units by completing courses
- **Provider completion certificates** — branded, printable proof of completion for licensing records
- **Course audit trail** — HIPAA-compliant logs of who accessed what and when

***

## B2B \& Practice Licensing

- **Employer wellness packages** — bulk seat access sold to companies for employee mental health programs
- **Practice portal** — each licensed psychiatric practice gets its own isolated environment with their branding, their providers, and their patients
- **White-label option** — practices can brand the platform as their own patient education tool
- **BAA support** — each practice signs a Business Associate Agreement (Google Cloud handles the infrastructure compliance when you migrate)[^5]
- **Revenue share model** — \$5,500 license fee + 60/40 split already in your financial model[^2]

***

## Notifications \& Engagement

- **Email reminders** — Resend integration already in your stack; automated nudges when assignments are due or a patient goes quiet[^3]
- **Provider alerts** — notify when a patient flags distress in a self-assessment
- **Newsletter campaigns** — re-engagement sequences for inactive users[^2]

***

## Safety \& Crisis Features

- **Crisis resource module** — always-visible link to 988, Crisis Text Line, and local resources
- **Distress flag system** — if a self-assessment response crosses a clinical threshold, it surfaces a prompt to contact their provider or call a crisis line
- **No AI diagnosis** — all content framed as education, never clinical assessment

***

## Infrastructure \& Compliance

- **HIPAA-compliant data handling** — encrypted at rest and in transit, audit logs, minimal PII[^3]
- **GCP multi-tenant architecture** — each practice gets isolated Cloud SQL + Storage when you migrate; one BAA per tenant[^5]
- **Anonymous user support** — public learners never required to share identifying information
- **Role-based access** — public user / patient / provider / practice admin / platform admin

***

This is your complete platform. Everything above is buildable on your existing Next.js + PostgreSQL stack — no third-party LMS needed, no platform fees, no feature gates. You own all of it.
