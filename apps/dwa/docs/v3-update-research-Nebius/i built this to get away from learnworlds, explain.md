# i built this to get away from learnworlds, explain in plain english what you mean

Got it — you built your **own custom platform** at `mental-health-education.soloframehub.com` using Next.js, React, PostgreSQL, and Tailwind CSS specifically to escape the limitations of LearnWorlds. Here's everything reframed in plain English.[^1][^2]

***

## What You Already Have

You've built a custom-coded mental health education website from scratch. It has courses on topics like OCD, bipolar disorder, anger management, and burnout. It's designed for both the public and patients from Real Psychiatric Services, with plans for a free tier and paid subscriptions. The payments side is intentionally turned off during testing right now.[^3][^4][^5][^2]

***

## What "Integrated Therapy" Actually Means in Plain English

Instead of just selling courses people watch alone, you'd be building **tools that therapists can use *with* their clients** — making your platform part of the actual therapy process, not just a supplement to it.

Here's how that breaks down across the three modes you mentioned:

**One-on-One (individual therapy)**
A therapist assigns specific lessons or exercises to a patient between sessions — like homework. The therapist can see what the patient completed, what they scored on self-assessments, and use that as a starting point for their next appointment. Your platform becomes part of their clinical workflow.

**Group therapy**
A therapist runs a structured 6–8 week program (like a DBT skills group or anxiety management group) where all participants work through the same course together. The platform hosts the curriculum, discussion prompts, and group check-ins — and the therapist facilitates live sessions using your content as the backbone.

**Online / self-directed**
This is what you already have — someone buys access and goes through a course on their own. The upgrade here is adding more interactivity: branching scenarios, reflection journals, skill-practice exercises, and progress badges.

***

## The Big Marketability Moves (Plain English)

- **Provider portal** — Give therapists their own login where they can assign courses to clients and track progress. This is the single biggest upgrade that takes you from "education site" to "clinical tool"[^3]
- **Certify the courses for CEUs** — Therapists need continuing education hours every year. If your courses count toward that, providers will *pay* to use them and refer clients[^3]
- **B2B employer packages** — Companies buy bulk access for their employees. This is where your projected revenue model hits scale — your 5-year projections already account for this channel[^5]
- **GCP migration with per-practice isolation** — When you license to other psychiatric practices, each gets their own secure environment and signs a BAA with Google. That's your HIPAA compliance story at scale[^2]

The core idea is simple: **right now the platform teaches people. The next version helps providers treat people.** That's the upgrade that unlocks the bigger revenue tiers you already projected.
