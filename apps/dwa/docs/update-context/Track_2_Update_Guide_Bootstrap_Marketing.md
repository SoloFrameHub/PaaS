## WHY BOOTSTRAP MARKETING IS UNIVERSAL

Bootstrap marketing is about **resource-constrained customer acquisition** - this reality applies to:
- **B2B founders** selling software without VC funding
- **Creator founders** building coaching/course businesses without ad budgets
- **Solo consultants** transitioning to productized offerings

The fundamental constraint is the same: **Time instead of money, organic instead of paid.**
