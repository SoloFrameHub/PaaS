## Critical Safety Considerations (from Research Brief)

✅ **Scope boundaries enforced:**
- Can: Notice, approach, listen non-judgmentally, provide psychoeducation, encourage referral, recognize crisis, escalate
- Cannot: Diagnose, provide therapy, recommend medications, conduct clinical risk assessment, promise confidentiality in crisis

✅ **ALGEE framework:** Applied consistently throughout (Approach, Listen, Give information, Encourage professional help, Encourage self-help)

✅ **Mandatory escalation criteria:** Clear guidance for active suicidal crisis, psychosis with danger, overdose, severe intoxication, threat to others

✅ **Safe messaging:** No method details, no glorification, no simplification of causes, emphasis on help available and recovery possible

✅ **Vicarious trauma:** Addressed in L17 with evidence-based prevention strategies (40-85% prevalence acknowledged)
