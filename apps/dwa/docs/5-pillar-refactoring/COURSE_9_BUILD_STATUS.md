### Component Usage Patterns

**Use in EVERY lesson:**
- Opening Callout (reflection or info) — sets tone
- At least 1 InsightGrid OR EnhancedAccordion — organizes complex info
- At least 1 FlipCard set OR InteractiveScenario — active engagement
- StepByStep for Key Takeaways — structured summary
- 2 Checkins at end: scale (helpfulness) + reflect (application) — feedback infrastructure

**Use strategically (not all in one lesson):**
- SlideNavigation — for sequential concept building (2-4 slides)
- BodyMap — for embodied awareness (use in 1-2 lessons max)
- Multiple EnhancedAccordions — for categorizing detailed content
- Multiple InteractiveScenarios — for decision-making practice (aim for 1 per lesson in lessons 5-20)
- Callout (warning) — for safety-critical content (lessons 3, 13, 14, 15, 16)

### Evidence Integration

**Each lesson must:**
- State evidence grade (STRONG, MODERATE-STRONG, etc.)
- Cite specific studies from research brief
- Include effect sizes or ORs/RRs where available
- Acknowledge limitations and caveats
- Use evidence-accurate language (avoid overstatement)

### Safety Integration

**Lessons requiring explicit safety content:**
- Lesson 3: Recreational vs. competitive distinction
- Lesson 13: Youth elite sport mental health risks
- Lesson 14: Access barriers (framing universal accessibility as exclusionary)
- Lesson 15: Toxic team culture recognition + when to leave
- Lesson 16: Exercise compulsion warning signs

**Safety language pattern:**
```markdown
<Callout type="warning" title="[Safety Issue]">
[Explanation of risk]

[Referral criteria or protective action]
</Callout>
```
