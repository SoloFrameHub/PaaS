## Module 1: Foundation & Science (Lessons 1-4)

### Lesson 1: The Science of Social Connection — Why Relationships Matter for Your Health
**Duration:** 30 min  
**Evidence Grade:** STRONG  
**Content:** Loneliness epidemic (HR=1.14 mortality), Surgeon General 2023 advisory, structural factors, shame-avoidance cycle

**Duration:** 30 min  
**Evidence Grade:** STRONG (attachment theory)

### Lesson 3: Quality vs. Quantity — Understanding Dunbar's Number & Relationship Layers
**Duration:** 25 min  
**Evidence Grade:** MODERATE-STRONG

### Lesson 4: Barriers to Connection & Overcoming Obstacles
**Duration:** 30 min

## Safety Notes

**CRITICAL Safety Boundaries:**
1. This course addresses **subclinical social anxiety + loneliness** — NOT clinical Social Anxiety Disorder (SAD)
2. SAD requires clinical CBT with exposure (NICE CG159) — self-guided courses insufficient
3. Explicit referral criteria required in Lesson 2/4/8/11
4. Structural barriers acknowledgment (Lesson 1/4) — loneliness is NOT solely individual skill deficit
