THE 60-DAY SPRINT

From Idea to Revenue

A structured path through validation, building, and launch. Each phase builds on the previous, eliminating the guesswork that kills most startups.

I

Validation Phase

Prove your idea has a market before writing a line of code. The PROOF framework gives you systematic validation with real market evidence—not opinions from friends and family.

II

Building Phase

Construct your MVP with the right architecture from day one. The STACK method ensures you're building something that can scale—without over-engineering for problems you don't have yet.

III

Launch Phase

Get your first paying customers. The LAUNCH framework takes you from "almost ready" to actually in market, with systematic approaches to finding and converting early adopters.

IV

Optimization Phase

Turn early traction into repeatable growth. Learn the feedback loops and iteration cycles that separate startups that stall from startups that scale.
