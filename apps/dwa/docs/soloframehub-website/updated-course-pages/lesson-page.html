01

Introduction & Context

5 min • The mindset shift

Most founders treat discovery calls like interrogations. They fire off a checklist of features hoping something sticks. This is the fastest way to lose a prospect.

In this lesson, we shift the dynamic. You are not selling; you are diagnosing. Just as a doctor doesn't prescribe surgery without an MRI, you cannot prescribe your solution without understanding the true pain.

Roadmap: We will cover the psychological framework (DISC) first, then move immediately into simulated practice.
02

The DISC Framework

15 min • Buyer Psychology

Identify your prospect's personality type within the first 2 minutes to tailor your communication style.

Dominance (D)

Direct, firm, results-oriented. Wants the bottom line.

Approach

Be brief. Focus on ROI. Don't ramble.

"Lead with '20% cost reduction in 90 days' rather than features."

Influence (I)

Outgoing, enthusiastic, optimistic. Loves storytelling.

Approach

Build rapport. Let them talk. Use visuals.

"Ask: 'Who else on your team would be excited about this vision?'"

Steadiness (S)

Even-tempered, accommodating, patient. Dislikes risk.

Approach

Provide assurances. Slow down. Show support.

"Say: 'We have a dedicated support team to walk you through every step.'"

Conscientiousness (C)

Analytical, reserved, precise. Values accuracy.

Approach

Use data. Be specific. Answer details thoroughly.

"Provide: 'Here is the case study with N=500 data points you asked for.'"
