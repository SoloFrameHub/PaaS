## 3. Design the revenue model for subscription and benefits

You want two intertwined value props: (1) recurring revenue for you via licensing, and (2) recurring or per-capita revenue for licensees via memberships or benefits.

## A. Licensing models you can offer

- Per‑seat subscription: a set number of active users (patients/clients) with monthly or annual pricing (e.g., 50 active users per month).

    - Starter (solo/1–3 clinicians)
    - Clinic (up to X clinicians; more users; branded portal)

For employers/EAPs:

- Per‑eligible‑employee per month (PEPM) pricing: common in the benefits space, where every eligible employee has access whether or not they use it.totemsolutions+2
