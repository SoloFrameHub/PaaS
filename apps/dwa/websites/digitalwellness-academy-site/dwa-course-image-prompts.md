### DWA-001 — Understanding & Managing Anxiety
`dwa-001-understanding-managing-anxiety.jpg`
**Palette: Cool slate blue and silver mist**

A woman sits cross-legged on a wooden dock extending over a still lake at early 
dawn, eyes closed, palms open on her knees. The water is a deep slate blue-grey, 
silver mist rising slowly from the surface. Storm clouds retreat to one edge of 
the sky while the first pale light of dawn — cool white, not yet warm — appears 
on the opposite horizon. Her posture is calm and deliberate against the fading 
storm. Color palette: cool greys, slate blue, silver, pale dawn light. 
Cinematic photorealism, shallow depth of field. 16:9.
