## Hero paragraph

> A 10-lesson psychoeducation course on the pattern commonly called codependency — over-responsibility for others' feelings, difficulty identifying one's own needs, and relational enmeshment — framed through the better-operationalized research construct of Differentiation of Self (Bowen Family Systems), together with assertiveness research and DBT interpersonal effectiveness skills. A 2022 scoping review of 295 primary studies found Differentiation of Self predicts psychological health, relationship quality, and intergenerational functioning. The course teaches these ideas as a framework for self-reflection and skill practice — not as therapy.
