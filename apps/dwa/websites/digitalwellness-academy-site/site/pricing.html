Licensing FAQ

Is there a free trial?

Not in the self-serve sense, but yes in a meaningful way. Beta applicants receive a 30-day sandbox with a provider account, patient test account, the marketing atomization demo, and direct access to our clinical team. No payment taken until you sign a license agreement.

How long does a license run?

License terms are negotiated based on your practice size, patient volume, and licensing structure. Contact us to discuss what fits your situation.

What's the BAA posture?

We sign a BAA with every licensed practice. Production deployments target BAA-compliant cloud infrastructure (GCP, AWS, or Azure) as a prerequisite for live patient use. Staging/demo environments are on Dokploy-managed infra for sandbox testing only.

Can I customize the content?

Light customization (practice branding, addenda, practice-specific notes) is included in every tier. Deeper custom course authoring is available as professional services. Our clinical content editors can author custom lessons aligned to your practice's specialties.

What happens to my patients' data if we stop working together?

You get an export in a structured format (JSON + CSV) of all your patient outcome data. Your white-label site and branding come down. Patients are notified per your licensing agreement's offboarding procedure. No lock-in on data.
