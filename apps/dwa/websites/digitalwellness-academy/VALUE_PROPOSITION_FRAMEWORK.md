# Digital Wellness Academy — Value Proposition Framework

**Purpose**: Translate technical features into user outcomes and business value for website copy.

**Rule**: Website copy should answer "What does this do for me?" not "How does this work?"

---

## Core Value Propositions by Audience

### For Mental Health Practices

#### Problem → Solution → Outcome

**PROBLEM**: Patients need support between 50-minute weekly sessions, but practitioners can't scale 1:1 time.

**SOLUTION**: Digital education + engagement platform that extends care 24/7 without adding provider workload.

**OUTCOME**:
- Better patient outcomes (structured skill-building between sessions)
- Recurring revenue stream ($16K-$36K/year per 100 patients at 70% split)
- Content marketing engine (courses → blog posts, emails → patient acquisition)
- Reduced no-shows (engaged patients show up)
- Crisis safety net (real-time alerts when patients need you)

#### Value Statements (Use These on Website)

✅ **"Extend patient care beyond the session without adding hours to your day"**  
❌ NOT: "DistilBERT-powered distress classifier with 95% sensitivity"

✅ **"Add $18K-$36K annual recurring revenue per 100 patients"**  
❌ NOT: "Revenue share model with 70/30 split via Polar.sh and Stripe infrastructure"

✅ **"Know when patients need you most with real-time crisis alerts"**  
❌ NOT: "AI-powered distress detection using fine-tuned DistilBERT classifier"

✅ **"Assign courses like homework, track progress like you're in the room"**  
❌ NOT: "Provider portal with patient assignment API and JSONB progress tracking"

✅ **"Turn your courses into blog posts, emails, and social content for patient acquisition"**  
❌ NOT: "Content atomization classifier extracts standalone marketing assets from lesson MDX files"

**NOT**: "Platform Features" (technical)

1. **"Extend Care Beyond the Session"**
   - Problem: 50-minute sessions aren't enough
   - Solution: Patients get 24/7 access to structured skill-building
   - Outcome: Better outcomes, reduced no-shows, higher satisfaction

2. **"Add Recurring Revenue"**

   - Solution: Subscription model with built-in revenue split
   - Outcome: $16K-$36K/year per 100 patients (at 70% split)

3. **"Grow Your Patient Base"**
   - Problem: Patient acquisition is expensive
   - Solution: Courses → blog posts, emails, SEO → organic traffic
   - Outcome: Content marketing engine drives new patient inquiries

4. **"Know When Patients Need You"**
   - Problem: Can't monitor patients between sessions
   - Solution: Real-time crisis detection and alerts
   - Outcome: Proactive outreach, improved safety, fewer crises

5. **"Guide Learning Like Homework"**
   - Problem: Therapy homework gets forgotten
   - Solution: Assign courses, track progress, see completion
   - Outcome: Patients stay accountable, skills transfer improves

6. **"Deploy Under Your Brand"**
   - Problem: Third-party tools lose your brand identity
   - Solution: White-label customization (logo, colors, domain)
   - Outcome: Maintain patient relationship, strengthen brand

## Tone & Messaging Guidelines

### DO:
- ✅ Lead with outcomes ("extend care," "add revenue," "grow practice")
- ✅ Use plain language ("courses you can assign like homework")
- ✅ Quantify value ("$16K-$36K/year," "10-20x higher engagement")
- ✅ Focus on user benefits ("learn by doing," "get help 24/7")
- ✅ Use active voice ("extend care" not "care can be extended")
- ✅ Show proof ("592 lessons," "HIPAA-compliant," "proven model")

### DON'T:
- ❌ Lead with technology ("DistilBERT," "fine-tuning," "Metabase")
- ❌ Use jargon ("JSONB," "MDX components," "RAG")
- ❌ Bury value in features ("provider portal with patient assignment API")
- ❌ Assume buyer knows mental health tech ("ERP," "CBT-I frameworks")
- ❌ Overclaim ("AI-powered transformation," "revolutionary platform")
- ❌ Use passive voice ("this platform enables practices to...")

## Copy Testing Checklist

Before publishing ANY page, ask:

1. ✅ **Can I understand the value in 10 seconds?** (Hero test)
2. ✅ **Does it answer "What's in it for me?"** (Buyer perspective)
3. ✅ **Are outcomes prioritized over features?** ("Add revenue" before "revenue share API")
4. ✅ **Is the language plain, not jargon?** (No "DistilBERT," "JSONB," "RAG")
5. ✅ **Do stats prove value?** ("$16K-$36K/year" not just "revenue share available")
6. ✅ **Is the CTA clear and specific?** ("Book a 30-min demo" not "Get started")
7. ✅ **Would a non-technical buyer understand this?** (Practice owner, HR director, platform PM)
