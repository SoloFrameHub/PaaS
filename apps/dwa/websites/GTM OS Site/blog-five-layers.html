Five Layers, One OS: The Architecture Behind GTM OS

Most platforms are a course with extras. GTM OS is five integrated layers - each one feeds the next.

Why layers matter

Course platforms bolt features on. A course ships. Then a community tab gets added. Then a certificate. Then maybe a tool or two. Each addition is independent - they don't talk to each other, don't share state, and don't compound.

GTM OS was architected differently from the start. Five layers, each with a specific job, and each layer's output becomes the next layer's input. What you learn in Layer 1 shapes what you practice in Layer 2. What you build in Layer 2 gets deployed in Layer 3. How you perform in Layer 3 surfaces in Layer 5. The layers compound because they're connected - not bolted together after the fact.

Layer 1: Learn

Learn LAYER 1
  • - 49 courses, 487 lessons, 487 quizzes across 7 tracks - Foundations, Sales Methodology, Marketing Engine, AI Acquisition, Creator Track, Operations and Systems, Customer Success.
  • - 165,000+ words of original curriculum. Not repurposed blog posts or AI-generated filler - purpose-built for B2B founders and their teams.
  • - Smart onboarding. A 4-step intake analyzes your business context - stage, industry, goals, bottlenecks - and personalizes your learning path before you open your first lesson.
  • - Free 98,000-word book bundled with the platform, with RAG-powered semantic search so you can ask questions and get answers grounded in the actual text.

Layer 2: Practice

Practice LAYER 2
  • - Voice-enabled AI roleplay against 4 DISC buyer types - Dominant, Influential, Steady, Conscientious - across 80+ industries. Practice discovery, demos, objection handling, and closing conversations before they happen in real deals.
  • - Versioned artifact builders. ICP document, positioning statement, email sequences, discovery playbook, objection library - each one versioned so you can track how your thinking evolves.
  • - Interactive workshops that pull in your actual business data from the onboarding intake - so exercises reflect your real market, not a generic example.

Layer 3: Execute

Execute LAYER 3
  • - Kanban pipeline board with deal tracking - stage, value, next action, last touch - so your live pipeline sits inside the same OS you learned from.
  • - Outreach log by channel - email, LinkedIn, phone, WhatsApp - with timestamps and outcome tracking for each touchpoint.
  • - 6 BYOK integrations: Attio, Pipedrive, Hunter.io, Notion, Brevo, WhatsApp Business. Your own API keys. No SaaS tax. Nothing stored on our servers.
  • - ICP Builder workbench with enrichment - combine Hunter.io email finding with Attio firmographic data to qualify and contact prospects without leaving the OS.

Layer 4: Connect

Connect LAYER 4
  • - DISC-matched accountability pods of 4-6 founders - matched on communication style and business stage so pod dynamics are productive rather than generic.
  • - NodeBB-powered community forum with structured categories, voting, activity feed, and member directory - not a Discord server with pinned posts.
  • - Automated n8n facilitator rhythm. Monday kickoff posts, Wednesday mid-week nudges, Friday synthesis - generated per-pod by AI and posted automatically so no pod goes quiet.
  • - Milestone celebrations posted to pods automatically when members hit achievements - so the community sees real progress, not just announcements.

Layer 5: Measure

Measure LAYER 5
  • - Context-aware AI coach with RAG search across your documents and the full lesson library - answers are grounded in both your business context and the curriculum, not generic advice.
  • - 5-dimension readiness tracking with before/after deltas - so you can see which competency areas moved and which are still gaps after each learning cycle.
  • - AI coaching nudges with specific daily recommendations - not generic "keep going" messages, but next-action suggestions based on your actual progress data.
  • - 40+ achievement badges across 4 tiers, with Badgr Open Badges 3.0 certification - shareable credentials that live outside the platform.
  • - Chatwoot live support built into the platform for real-time help when you're stuck - not a chatbot queue.
