Pipeline · State Machine

Deals advance when diagnostic criteria are confirmed. Not before.

Lead → Contacted → Meeting → Proposal → Won / Lost. Each stage advances only when the PAIN → IMPACT → DECISION diagnostic is met. No more deals rotting in "Proposal Sent" because nothing was checking whether pain was actually quantified and decision authority was confirmed.

Bi-directional sync with your CRM of choice - the OS is the source of truth for methodology; your CRM stays the source of truth for your team. Both in lockstep.

  • →Seven channels logged per deal - Email · LinkedIn · Phone · Twitter · WhatsApp · Event · Other
  • →Five actions tracked - Initial Outreach · Follow-up · Meeting Booked · Meeting Held · Proposal Sent
  • →Four outcome states - Positive · Neutral · Negative · Pending - 7-day dashboard per channel
  • →Loss-reason tracking surfaces which objections actually close out deals - the coach flags stale deals automatically

DISC-Matched Cohort Pods

Not a Discord. An engineered community.

4–6 founders matched on five algorithmic dimensions - DISC diversity, deal-size clustering, industry mix, curriculum stage proximity, pod fill ratio - with a weekly facilitator rhythm that runs on autopilot. Four AI personas participate alongside humans: Alex (D · Skeptic - demands data, challenges vague claims) · Jordan (I · Builder - action-oriented encouragement) · Morgan (C · Perfectionist - detail-focused feedback) · Sam (S · Mentor - supportive guidance).

Persona count adapts to human count: 1 human → 3 personas · 2 humans → 2 personas · 3+ humans → 1 persona (facilitator only). As engagement grows, bots fade out. The community becomes human-led on its own schedule.

Mon

Kickoff

AI facilitator opens the week with a prompt tied to the pod's current curriculum stage. Members share what they're working on + what's in their way.

Wed

Nudge

Mid-week engagement check. Surfaces a discussion question relevant to the pod's progress. Celebrates wins posted since Monday.

Fri

Synthesis

The week wraps with a summary of what was discussed, explicit callouts to members who moved, and a preview of next week's theme.

Plus milestone auto-celebrations when a pod member completes a course, scores ≥80 on a roleplay, closes their first deal, or hits a revenue milestone. The community sees your wins as they happen.
