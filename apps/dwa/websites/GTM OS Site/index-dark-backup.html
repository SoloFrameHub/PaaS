Solo Growth via AI and Retention

Approximately 20 minutes · Two researchers in conversation

Position 1 · Retention as the compounding moat

Sustainable growth lives in post-sale retention, onboarding psychology, and LTV math — not in the acquisition sprint.

→ A 5% retention lift drives 25–95% profit gains.
→ The first 48 hours define trust; a "time to value" under 15 minutes beats buyer's remorse.
→ Onboarding + LTV math compound; acquisition spend doesn't.
Position 2 · Acquisition rigor as the survival gate

Without systematic front-end qualification, retention metrics are theoretical — you don't have customers to retain.

→ 50% of prospects are unqualified; 40–60% of B2B deals die in "no decision" from status-quo bias.
→ Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the new visibility layer in an AI-driven search landscape.
→ Minimum Viable Qualification (MVQ) — Pain, Impact, Decision — protects the founder's most limited resource: time.

The GTM OS answer: both. The platform runs MVQ and AEO at the qualification gate and the context-aware coach plus 5-signal Living Curriculum loop at the retention stage — same runtime, both halves of the growth equation.
