Why we built a certification

The GTM OS is free during beta. The curriculum spans 49 courses across 7 tracks covering everything from ICP definition to pipeline management to AI-powered outreach. But comprehensive does not automatically mean credible - anyone can say they "completed" a course.

We wanted a credential that meant something to a potential investor, partner, client, or employer reviewing a founder's profile. Something that signals: this person can execute a complete go-to-market strategy from scratch, solo, and has the artifacts to prove it.

The certification is not a badge for showing up. It represents demonstrated competency across every dimension of GTM execution.

The full requirements

1

Complete all 49 courses across 7 tracks

Foundations (ICP, positioning, acquisition paths, list building), Marketing Engine (content, SEO, LinkedIn, cold email, automation), Sales Methodology (DISC personas, discovery, objection handling, closing, pipeline), AI-Powered Acquisition (AI strategy, deliverability, research, outreach, autonomous SDRs), Creator Economy (audience conversion, webinars, DM selling, community-led), Customer Success (onboarding, retention, expansion, advocacy), and Operations (CRM, analytics, automation, scaling, legal, finance, capstone).

2

Pass all assessments with 80% or higher

Assessments are AI-graded, not multiple choice. The AI evaluates your strategic thinking, the specificity of your answers, and how well you apply frameworks to your actual business context. Scoring below 80% on any assessment means retaking it until you demonstrate real understanding.

3

Build all 10 artifact types

ICP Document, Positioning Statement, Value Proposition Canvas, Acquisition Path, List-Building Criteria, DISC Profile, Discovery Playbook, Objection Library, Email Sequences, and Personal Sales Playbook. Every artifact must be saved and versioned in the platform. These are not worksheets - they are operational documents for your business.

4

Complete at least 3 AI roleplay sessions with passing scores (75%+)

The roleplay simulator evaluates you across 12 dimensions: question quality, talk-time ratio, objection handling, authority positioning, discovery depth, and more. Sessions support three methodologies (MEDDIC, SPIN, CHALLENGER) and adapt difficulty based on your DISC profile. Scores are AI-graded in real time. This is where theory becomes practice.

5

Participate in at least one cohort sprint

Join a DISC-matched pod of up to 6 founders and complete a structured sprint. Post artifacts for peer review, give feedback to others, and engage with the AI facilitator's weekly rhythm. GTM does not mean isolated GTM - you need to demonstrate you can collaborate and pressure-test your strategy with peers.

What makes this different from other certifications

EVIDENCE-BASED

Your artifacts ARE the proof. Your ICP document, discovery playbook, objection library, and email sequences are not theoretical exercises - they are operational documents built for your specific business. The certification links directly to your artifact portfolio, so anyone verifying it can see exactly what you built.

AI-EVALUATED

There are no multiple-choice exams. Every assessment is AI-graded, evaluating the depth of your strategic thinking and how well you apply frameworks to real scenarios. The roleplay simulator scores you on 12 dimensions across discovery calls, objection handling, and closing conversations. The AI does not care if you memorized the textbook - it evaluates whether you can execute.

PRACTITIONER-BUILT

The curriculum was created by someone who built the GTM system and used it, not by an academic who studied GTM theory. The 49 courses reflect real execution patterns - what actually works when you are a founder with no sales team, no marketing department, and no budget for enterprise tools.

VERSIONED PORTFOLIO

Your certification links to your actual artifact portfolio inside the platform. Each artifact stores the last 5 versions, timestamped and course-associated. Anyone who verifies your credential can see not just that you completed the work, but how your thinking evolved as you progressed through the curriculum. This is a living portfolio, not a static PDF.

Career and business value

The certification is designed to be useful beyond the platform. Here is what it signals and why that matters.

Signal to clients and employers

The credential tells potential clients, investors, partners, or employers that you can execute a complete GTM strategy solo - from ICP research through pipeline management to closing. It is verifiable on LinkedIn with one click.

Portfolio of real business artifacts

Your certification links to 10 artifact types you actually built for your business. This is not a PDF of slide decks or a transcript of lectures watched. It is a versioned collection of operational documents - ICP analyses, discovery playbooks, email sequences, objection libraries - that demonstrate real strategic thinking.

Ongoing value post-certification

Your artifacts continue to evolve after certification. The platform stores the last 5 versions of each artifact, so your ICP document, playbook, and sequences get sharper over time. The credential represents a baseline of competency, and the portfolio shows continued growth.

How it is issued

We use Badgr, which implements the Open Badges 3.0 standard. When you meet all five criteria, the OS calls the Badgr API and issues a badge assertion to your email. The assertion is a verifiable credential - not an image, but a cryptographically signed record tied to your account.

From the completion screen, one click pre-fills the LinkedIn "Add Certification" form with your credential URL, issue date, and our organization ID. LinkedIn then displays it on your profile under Licenses & Certifications, where anyone can click through to verify it.

We will migrate to Credly when volume exceeds 1,000 badges - Credly has better employer recognition at scale. For now, Badgr is free, fast, and standards-compliant.

Note: Trademark and certification mark registration for the Certified GTM Practitioner credential is in progress.

The /certified directory

There is a public /certified page that lists certified founders by name, company, and optional revenue milestone. It is opt-in - you choose whether to appear. The goal is to build a credible reference list of founders who have demonstrably done the GTM work, not just studied it.

Spanish version

The Spanish certification is called Profesional Certificado en GTM. It uses the same five criteria applied to the Spanish curriculum (Track 1 ES is complete; remaining tracks are in translation). The badge is a distinct design with the ES label and issues through the same Badgr infrastructure.

Timeline

Serious members working 10-15 hours per week can expect to reach certification in 4-6 months. The curriculum is self-paced, so faster is possible, but the requirement to build all 10 artifacts and pass AI-evaluated assessments means you cannot speed-run it by skimming. The roleplay sessions and cohort sprint require scheduling with real people and real AI evaluations.

During beta, all curriculum and certification are free. Post-beta, certification will be included in the standard subscription.
