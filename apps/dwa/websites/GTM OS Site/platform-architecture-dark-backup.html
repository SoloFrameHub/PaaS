The Problem I Kept Running Into

Wrestling with SaaS Platforms

I built five courses on LearnWorlds using custom Interactive HTML5 lessons — coded in my editor, then pasted into their platform. It worked better than videos or static quizzes, but it exposed the fundamental problem:

I was spending more time wrestling with a proprietary SaaS than actually helping founders build their businesses.

Every content update meant navigating someone else's system. Every interactive element required workarounds.

Real-time AI coaching that generates usable artifacts, adapts to specific challenges, and measures actual business outcomes — not just lesson completions.

A customer acquisition Operating System that helps founders build while they learn — automating the workflow from prospect research to outreach to closing.
