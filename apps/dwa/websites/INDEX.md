## 🎨 Shared Design Principles

### Voice & Tone
- **Grounded, credible, founder-led** (not AI guru hype)
- **Applied AI for business** (not research/academic)
- **Specific, evidence-aware** (not vague "transform your X")
- **Reassuring, trauma-informed** where relevant (DWA clinical content)
- **B2B/product-focused** (not consumer marketing)

### Messaging Patterns
- **Clear category positioning** in 10 seconds
- **Outcome-focused value props** (revenue, engagement, time-to-deploy)
- **Build vs. buy framing** (for licensing/platform products)
- **"Powered by" cross-linking** (verticals → parent, parent → verticals)

### SEO Structure
- **Title tags**: 60 chars, keyword-first
- **Meta descriptions**: 160 chars, benefit-focused
- **H1**: Matches title intent, clear value prop
- **Schema markup**: Organization, SoftwareApplication, FAQPage
- **Internal linking**: Strategic cross-linking between sites
