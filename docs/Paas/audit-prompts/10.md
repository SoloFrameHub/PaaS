  RAG retrieval scoping.
- **`analytics/ga4-server`**: ensure the Measurement Protocol secret
  isn't logged.
- **`forms/`**: formSubmission service — validation, rate-limit,
  anti-spam.
- **`prompts/`**: YAML / MD prompt files. Grep for embedded secrets in
  prompt templates.
- **`repositories/`**: any that deal with `subscription`,
  `bookPurchase`, `pipelineDeal` must enforce userId ownership.
